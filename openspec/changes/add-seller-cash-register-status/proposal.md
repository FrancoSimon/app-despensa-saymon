## Why

After opening a cash register, sellers return to the counter panel but do not have a visible shortcut to close the register. This interrupts the daily sales flow.

## What Changes

- Show the current cash register status on the seller counter panel.
- Link the seller directly to the cash register page to open or close the register.

## Impact

- Affected specs: `api-surface`.
- Affected files: `app/vendedor/page.tsx`, OpenSpec artifacts.

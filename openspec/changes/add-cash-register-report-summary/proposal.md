## Why

Reports show sales and payment totals, but cash register auditing now includes register closures and manual movements. Admins need a period-level view of expected cash, counted cash, differences, and movement totals.

## What Changes

- Add cash register summary metrics to admin reports.
- Show recent cash register shifts for the selected period.
- Show manual cash movement totals for the selected period.

## Impact

- Affected specs: `reports-analytics`.
- Affected files: `app/admin/reportes`, `lib/reports`, OpenSpec artifacts.

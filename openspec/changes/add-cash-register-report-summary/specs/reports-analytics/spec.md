## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Admin Reports

The system SHALL show business reports for sales, products, wholesale orders, low stock, and cash register auditing.

#### Scenario: Admin reviews cash register report
- **WHEN** an admin opens reports for a selected period
- **THEN** the system shows cash register count, expected cash, counted cash, cash difference, income movements, and withdrawal movements
- **AND** the system lists cash registers in the period with links to their detail pages

## Why

Large product catalogs and carts make the counter and wholesale screens harder to scan. Operators can already search or scan products, so the initial catalog should stay compact. Product category entry also needs suggested values to keep data cleaner while still allowing new categories.

## What Changes

- Limit initial product results in the counter terminal and wholesale portal.
- Keep search/category filtering available for finding more products.
- Make cart item lists more compact and scrollable in counter and wholesale flows.
- Add category suggestions to the admin product form while allowing custom category text.

## Impact

- Affected specs: api-surface
- Affected code: POS terminal, wholesale order terminal, product form
